EMILIA ISOBEL CLARKE

Emilia Isobel Euphemia Rose Clarke MBE (born 23 October 1986) is a British actress. She is best known for her portrayal of Daenerys Targaryen in the HBO fantasy series Game of Thrones (2011–2019), for which she received nominations for four Primetime Emmy Awards.
Clarke started to act in stage productions while attending school. She appeared in student productions of Twelfth Night and West Side Story while attending St Edward's School.After taking a sabbatical year, she was accepted into Drama Centre London. Clarke was cast in her third professional role in 2010, as Daenerys Targaryen in the HBO fantasy series Game of Thrones.Clarke received multiple award nominations and wins for her role of Daenerys. After the first season, Clarke won Best Supporting Actress in a Drama at the 2011 EWwy Awards. she reportedly became one of the highest-paid actors on television, earning between £1.2 and £2 million per episode of Game of Thrones.In addition to the television show, she lent her voice and likeness to the 2014 video game of the same name.She also made a cameo appearance during Kit Harington's monologue on Saturday Night Live in April 2019.
Clarke's first film role was in the short film "Shackled"(2012).The film was featured in the 2020 Amazon Prime Video horror anthology series Murder Manual.In 2013, she was cast as Sarah Connor in the science fiction action film Terminator Genisys (2015).The film, which also stars Arnold Schwarzenegger, Jai Courtney, and Jason Clarke, received unfavourable reviews from critics In 2022, Clarke was cast in the animation film The Amazing Maurice.In April 2021, Clarke joined the cast of Secret Invasion for Disney+, set in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.The show premiered on 21 June 2023.Clarke was voted the most desirable woman in the world by AskMen readers in 2014.In 2015, she was named Esquire's Sexiest Woman Alive and was also recognised with the GQ Woman of the Year Award.
